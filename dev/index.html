About

Atlas Does

  • Runs sophisticated title searches
  • Helps you get to content you can actually watch, like "I want to watch Glee and I am in the US"
  • Automatically produces all the content lists needed for an online video service, from episodes in a brand to a-to-z lists and the current highlights
  • Stores all you expect: title, description, image, thumbnail, genres, tags and many ways to play it
  • Keeps an eye on Twitter, indexing all content mentions and what's currently hot
  • Next to web, finds mobile and TV friendly content
  • Lets you ask for content by time, like "What's on the BBC right now?"
  • Outputs information in many different formats

Atlas Is

  • The open-source, open-API index that gathers and stores video & audio information from 10+ sources, so that you can build cool apps swiftly
  • The way to learn all about a content link, of to find the content link itself

Atlas Will

  • Figure equivalent content from different sources
  • Search across title, description, tags and genres
  • Add more and more sources, and improve and augment the data from the current ones
